Corporate Profile

Foundation and Specialization

Founded in 2019 in New Delhi, India, Renal World Healthcare Pvt Ltd specialises in healthcare for select human organs, particularly kidneys. We deliver a comprehensive and high-quality out-patient healthcare via a chain of super specialty clinics, dialysis centres and pharmacies.

Commitment to Quality

Aiming for excellence, our primary focus is delivering high-quality healthcare services at affordable prices in under-served regions. We are committed to providing a healthier and enriched life to our trusted patients and partners.

Innovation

We leverage modern technology for patient care, OPD patient management, and record-keeping. We are developing native AI applications for smoother patient diagnosis, treatment, support, and outreach.

Service Region

With headquarters in New Delhi, our focus area is spread across Eastern India, bridging a significant healthcare gap in the region. We routinely organise healthcare & public awareness camps in peripheral regions of Eastern India to deliver much needed quality healthcare services.

Team and Facilities

Our team consists of highly qualified medical professionals, data & technologists, managerial professionals, trained technicians, and paramedics, among others. Our team is committed to serving the utmost quality healthcare services and pioneering new ways (research) of providing affordable and timely healthcare to patients.

We are more than a healthcare provider; we are a movement founded on the core principles of empathy, equity, and excellence in renal health. Our integrated care model ensures every aspect of a patient's well-being is prioritized.
